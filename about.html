ABOUT ME

The aim of this brand is to inspire young people to follow and stand for what they be-
lieve in. To send messages of positivity. To establish TISANKHE as much more than a
mere design brand but a VOICE! To constantly learn and grow in my craft. To keep
pushing the skill and quality of work produced by the brand to new levels. The vision of
this brand is to be a sharer of knowledge in an expressive society. Lastly, to do all things
with grace and in love.
